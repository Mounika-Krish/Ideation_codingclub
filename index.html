Currently in Phase 1

OVERVIEW
We are calling all creative minds, problem solvers, the geeks and coders at heart to re-imagine the future with us.
THEME
Think and build impactful projects to make the day to day life better.
SUGGESTIONS
  • Apps

    • Build useful applications like ambulance tracker, Intelligent chatbots, stock market app with recommended buy-sell tips, etc.

  • Robotics and drones

    • Design drones and robots that can solve some of the pressing challenges of India such as handling medical emergencies, search and rescue operations, etc

  • Tourism

    • Ideas that can boost the current situation of the tourism industries including hotels, travel and others.

  • Financial services

    • Find a way to resolve billing disputes.

  • Healthcare

    • Find a way for monitoring patients and suggesting effective treatments.

  • Agriculture, Foodtech and rural development

    • Developing solutions, keeping in mind the need to enhance the primary sector of India - Agriculture and to manage and process our agricultural produce.

  • Surveillance

    • Find a way to enable more security in public places and surveillance solutions that detect abnormal activities and inform managers to intervene.

  • Textile and Fashion

    • Find a way for investigation and evaluation of fibers, yarns and textile quality.

  • Fitness and Sports

    • Ideas that can boost fitness activities and assist in keeping fit.

  • Open innovation

    • Convince us that your idea is here to create value for society.

Note: All your ideas should involve programming.
RULES
  • This is a team participation hackathon. The team size should be of 3 to 5 members (preferably members from different departments).
  • You can take any of the suggested domains, but your projects should include programming.
  • It is sufficient if one member in a team registers. Details of all the team members and project proposals have to be submitted during registration.
  • Your project proposal should be as per the guidelines mentioned in the template. Click here to download the project proposal template
  • Once the hackathon starts, you will get only one chance to submit your ideas.
  • Plagiarism will lead to disqualification of the team.
  • Failing to make the submissions on time will be liable to be rejected.
  • Your hack must be developed entirely during the Hackathon duration. You may use open source libraries and other freely available systems / services such as Google Maps, Facebook Connect, Twitter feeds etc.
  • The intellectual property of your code belongs only to your team.
TIMELINE

Phase 1 (Registration starts on 4th Sep, 2022 and ends on 12th Sep, 2022)

Registrations and Idea Submissions

Form a team of 3 to 5 members and submit your innovative ideas and solutions as per the project proposal template.

Phase 2 (Starts from 25th Sep, 2022)

Mid Review

Selected students have to present their prototype to the jury.

Final Phase ( Starts from 15th Oct, 2022)

Grand Finale

Compete in the grand finale and present your innovative ideas. Cash prizes will be given for top 3 projects
